SCE Customer Experiments Based on brief as on June 2021

The current hypothesis is that a single cloud based digital service, could meet user needs by allowing them to self-serve information about multiple benefits in one place.

Experiment 1

Experimental

Initial conceptual prototype to explore assumptions:

  • It is more convenient for a user to self-serve than wait in a telephone queue
  • That multi-benefit users would benefit from finding information in one place

Experiment 2

experimental

Iteration of initial conceptual prototype

Experiment 3

experimental

Conceptual prototype to test hypothesis

  • To prove the reverse hypothesis that people prefer to receive benefit from separate online services than a single place where everything is together so that we remove any unconscious bias in the first two experiments.

Experiment 4

experimental

Conceptual prototype to test hypothesis

  • To prove the reverse hypothesis that people prefer to receive benefit from separate online services than a single place where everything is together so that we remove any unconscious bias in the first two experiments.

  • We believe that providing payment, LoE and contact information to claimants having multiple benefits will decrease call volumes to DWP about payments and general information queries.
